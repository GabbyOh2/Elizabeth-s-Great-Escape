# Elizabeth-s-Great-Escape
This is based on an anime, Gintama.

## Game Overview
This is a survival game where Elizabeth is trying to escape the Stupid Prince who captured her due to deeming her as a "rare species". He wants to make her part of his collection of pets. Elizabeth, on the other hand, has other plans. As a member of the Joi Faction, she needs to take part of their future plans and must escape.  

## Controls
| Button | Action |
| ------ | ------ |
| **A Button** | Skip Dialogue |
| **D-Pad LEFT** | Elizabeth moves left |
| **D-Pad RIGHT** | Elizabeth moves right |
| **D-Pad UP** | Elizabeth moves up |
| **D-Pad DOWN** | Elizabeth moves down |
| **START Button** | Start/Skip Dialogue |
| **SELECT Button** | Pause |
| **B Button** | Instructions |
| **LSHOULDER Button** | cheat |


## How to Play
- **Objective**: Elizabeth must press buttons in a certain order to initiate an attack against the "pets" the stupid prince sent after her in attempt to re-capture her.

### Game Mechanics
- **Dodging + Pressing Buttons**: left/right/up/dpwn buttons allows the player to runaway from enemies and press buttons.
- **CHEAT** When Elizabeth is losing a life she can replenish it.

### Win Conditions
- **Win**: The player must kill all enemies without dying. 
- **Lose**: The player dies without killing all enemies.
